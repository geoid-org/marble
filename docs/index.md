---


**Meta Marble** is a map dataset.

The map dataset contains vector representations of:

- Grid Systems, with a focus on DGGS (Discrete Global Grid System)
- Ecoregions
- Timezones


---


**Meta Marble** is dedicated to __The Blue Marble__ is an image of Earth taken on December 7, 1972, from a distance of around 29,000 kilometers (18,000 miles) from the planet's surface. Taken by the crew of the Apollo 17 spacecraft on its way to the Moon, it is one of the most reproduced images in history.
